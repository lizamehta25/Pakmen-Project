Pakmen Volleyball

Role

Social Media Coordinator

Tools

Adobe CC, Figma

Timeline

Jun 2023 - Aug 2023

Overview

Pakmen Volleyball is a prominent organization that focuses on creating a dynamic and engaging community for volleyball enthusiasts. As the Social Media Coordinator, I contributed to managing event promotions, creating visually appealing content, and ensuring timely communication across various platforms. This role required a strong understanding of digital media tools and an ability to effectively engage with the target audience. The experience was incredibly fulfilling and helped me grow as a professional in the social media space.

My Work

UNDERSTANDING THE BRAND

After my first week at Pakmen, I realized the club stood out from others in the GTA by supporting youth from all backgrounds in an engaging way, regardless of skill level. The club brought people together through their shared passion for volleyball, and not only gave youth a physical outlet but also provided mentors to support their personal growth. I focused on building a connection with the audience and used storytelling to showcase the club’s diversity, unique programs, and strong sense of community, with the goal of attracting more people to the Pakmen courts.

SHOWCASING THE POSSIBILITIES

I started off by showing the team what was possible by creating a mockup of an overlapping Instagram grid and recreating common posts. I focused on developing their brand by creating a cohesive colour scheme, typography, and dynamic graphics and photos.

The Results

CONTENT

I gathered footage at the Pakmen courts and summer camps to create Instagram reels using CapCut. I referenced popular short form media trends at the time while putting my own twist on them to make them relevant to Pakmen. I added memes, popular music, and pop culture references to further relate to youth and convey the message that Pakmen Volleyball is a fun place.

My most popular ideas included: What’s your volleyball ick? Why do you love Pakmen summer camp? I’m passing the phone to someone who…

STATISTICAL

The summer campaign brought a remarkable surge in user engagement on Pakmen’s social media platforms. This transformation led to an increase in summer camp registration and a significant increase in engagement. In July alone more than 8000 accounts were engaged with an 14.1% increase of followers and 113% non follower engagement. Ar the end of the summer, Pakmen received over 26,000 total content interactions and over 1000 new followers.

Key Takeaways

COMMUNITY OVER NUMBERS

One of the most rewarding outcomes of my social media campaign at Pakmen was the sense of community it fostered. When I first started, people were hesitant to participate, but as they began to see relatable content that resonated with them, they became more eager to get involved. By the end of the campaign, a sense of excitement had built up—people would gather around to watch the daily edits, parents would ask to have their kids featured, and a friendly competition developed between locations to submit the best content. None of this would have been possible if I hadn’t acted on the idea I had. I realized that taking initiative and bringing my talents to the table can lead to unexpected, positive results. This experience taught me the importance of stepping up and being proactive in creating opportunities for connection and engagement.
